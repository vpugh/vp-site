I'm Tori. I specialize in design & development for websites and emails; strategy for email campaigns and user-experience.

Developing successful products is more than just how good it looks. You need to do research, have a strategy, and utilize critical-thinking to create solutions that balance the desires of yourself and your customers'. These balanced solutions are what create great brands and satisfied customers. These solutions all start with design.

Design is problem-solving. Identifying the problem and finding the appropriate solution. It’s much more complex than just making things visually pleasing. My philosophy is critical listening and asking the right questions. These are the keys to developing great ideas/solutions. These tailor made solutions could be a website redesign; better utilization of email lists; a brand refresh; or whatever your needs are.
